About Us

Dear User,
Study to Shine is an Open Education platform that is committed to provide quality Open Educational Resources.The resources can be downloaded and shared alike. The content on the site is classified under five heads namely I Learn , I Enjoy , I Excel , I Explore ,My Gallery.
I Learn :The section contains learning resources in the form of video/audio lessons, assignments and assessments on various academic and non academic subjects.
I Enjoy: It makes learning fun as it contains games, simulations, walk through that makes learning enjoyable.
I Excel: In today's world, technology becomes the first toy of a child and favourite passtime for all ages leading us to live in a virtual world cut off from real relations and real learnings.I Excel section revists the essential life skills in an interesting way through case study approach.
I Explore: There are many resources that can significantly help the learning and development of children. This section aims to showcase such resources under a single heading for parents, teachers and children.
My Gallery: Every now and then we need to take printouts or use images, cliparts for projects. My Gallery contains classified images/cliparts mapped with the NCERT Curriculum for different classes.It also contains artwork such as posters, gifs that can be extremely useful for students.
It is suggested to create a login with the website to have a personalized experience.Having a login will also allow you to upload your creations/resources. The creations will be made public on the website under a relevant heading after plagiarism check and moderation with attribution.
